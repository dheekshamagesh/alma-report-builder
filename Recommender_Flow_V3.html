✦ What makes a good recommender?
✏️ Edit for this case ▲
  • • Mix collaborators with independents. USCIS weights independent expert recommenders more heavily, but collaborators speak to specific contributions better. Aim for ~60/40 independent.
  • • US-based preferred. Recommenders located in the US carry more weight than international ones for sustained-national-acclaim claims.
  • • Match recommender to criterion. A Fortune 500 CTO speaks to Critical Role credibly; an editor of a top journal speaks to Published Material. Avoid using the same person for everything.
  • • 5–7 recommenders is typical for an O-1A; some cases need fewer. The target adjusts per case.
